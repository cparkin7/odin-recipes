Creamy Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Another Italian Favorite, this next dish is a dish best kept simple. It is very easy to over-complicate the ever-popular Fettuccine Alfredo, so in this recipe we will keep it simple and let the ingredients do the work!

With just a handful of ingredients that are almost always available in the pantry or the fridge, this is a delicious dinner for any night of the week! Plus, you can add a variety of meats (my favorite is chicken!) to really spice the dish up. For a feels-good classic, you can't beat Fettuccine Alfredo!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. Add salt, pepper and garlic salt. Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted; this will thicken the sauce.
  3. Add pasta to sauce. Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
